
# Broth recipe

## Ingredients

- **1** onion
- **1** carrot
- **1** medium parsley
- **1/4** selery
- **3-5** leaves of leek
- **3** bay leaves
- **5** allspice seeds
- **salt and black pepper** to taste

## Instructions
1. Fry onion in the pot. 
2. Add 2l of water. 
3. Add a carrot, parsley, cellery, leek, bay leaf and all spice. 
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Boil untill the cellery, parsley and carrot are soft.
